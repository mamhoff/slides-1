What is "community"?

  • People
  • who meet
  • around something

Learning Communities

  • Your village is too small?
  • Your hobby/job so cool?
  • Teach others!

→ All communities are about passing knowledge

The golden triangle

Necessary Values

  • Respect
  • Inclusiveness
  • Empowerment

Codes of Conduct

  • Formalized values
  • Show your values
  • Enforce your values

Example: The Berlin Code of Conduct

Team

Organizing communities is work!

  • Meet regularly
  • Meet in person
  • Show some <3 ! Bring Snacks, Drinks

Baby Steps

  • Find a team (can take a month or two!)
  • Map out the environment (on a physical map!)
  • Plan your first event!

Your first event

  • Set an aim
  • Look for coaches and train them
  • Advertise! Social media, press, networks

This will take a month or even two!

Great expectations ...

... can seldomly be met.

  • Aim for what you can achieve
  • Every event is an iteration
  • You learn while doing it

Always set expectations so you can meet them

How to kill an event

  • Not regular
  • No fun/learning
  • Hungry/thirsty/tired participants
